⌨️  CONTROLS:

   Left Swipe       → Delete letter (hand gesture)

   ESC              → Quit

💡 TIPS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Ensure good lighting for best hand detection
• Keep hands within camera frame
• Wear contrasting colors (e.g., dark hands, light background)
• Hold letters steadily for ~1 second to register
• Swipe left smoothly for backspace

For more tips, see README.md
